# 🎓 CareerPath Karnataka

A full-stack career guidance platform designed to help students after PUC explore career paths, colleges, exams, and make informed decisions.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🔍 Search careers based on stream (Science, Commerce, Arts)
- 🏫 Explore colleges across Karnataka
- 📍 District-based filtering
- 📊 Career recommendations
- ⚡ Fast and responsive UI

---

## 🧠 Problem Statement

Students after PUC often face confusion in choosing the right career due to scattered information and lack of proper guidance.

---

## 💡 Solution

CareerPath Karnataka provides a centralized platform where students can:
- Explore career options
- Understand required exams
- Find top colleges
- Get structured guidance
